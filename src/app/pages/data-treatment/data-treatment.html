Data Treatment Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

1. Data Processing Scope

This policy outlines how we handle data processed by our AI solutions. We are committed to transparency regarding the lifecycle of your data, from collection to deletion.

2. Rights of Data Subjects

Under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Request the erasure of your data (Right to be Forgotten).
  • Restrict processing.

3. Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. AI conversation logs are anonymized or deleted after a set period unless otherwise agreed upon in a specific service contract.

4. Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your account or any personal data associated with our services, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our Data Protection Officer at rubens221001@gmail.com.
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line.
  3. Please specify if you want to delete specific data points or your entire account history.

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm once the deletion is complete.

5. Security Measures

We implement robust security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access or breaches.
